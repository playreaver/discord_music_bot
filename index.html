Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 14, 2025

1. Information Collection

The Bot may receive:

- Your Discord user ID and username/nickname to display requests.

- Server ID and channel ID where you use the Bot.

- Information about tracks being played (title, link, source).

Our website may collect:

- Your country and region (used to show relevant regional offers and content).

- Anonymous statistics on website usage (pages visited, time spent) using Yandex.Metrica to analyze and improve our service.

2. Information Usage

- For the Bot's functionality (music playback, queue management, track limits).

- To display messages and interact with you through commands and buttons.

- To provide you with relevant regional offers and content on our website.

- To analyze general website traffic and user behavior for service improvement.

3. Data Storage

- Data about tracks and server limits is stored in memory and not shared with third parties.

- Downloaded audio files are temporarily stored in the downloads folder and automatically deleted after playback.

- Data collected by Yandex.Metrica is stored in accordance with its privacy policy.

4. Information Sharing

- We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties.

- Data may only be used for the Bot's operation and supporting its functionality.

- Anonymous, aggregated data collected by Yandex.Metrica is used for internal analysis only.

5. Cookies and Tracking

- The Bot does not use cookies and does not track activity outside of Discord servers and voice channels.

- Our website uses Yandex.Metrica, which employs cookies and similar technologies to collect anonymous traffic data. You can learn more and opt-out on the Yandex.Metrica official page.

6. Policy Changes

- The developer may update the privacy policy.

- Continued use of the Bot or website after changes means acceptance of the new version.
